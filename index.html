What is PHRED?

Philomath High Robotics and Engineering Division (PHRED) is a set of teams based in Philomath, Oregon, comprised of middle and high school students who participate in the FIRST Robotics program.
The teams focus on designing, building, and programming robots to compete in various regional competitions.
Through hands-on experience, students develop skills in engineering, coding, and teamwork while tackling real-world challenges. PHRED Robotics provides an environment where students can explore their interests in science and technology, collaborate with peers, and learn from mentors in the field.
