Become a Sponsor!
The PHRED team is grateful for the support of our sponsors, whose generous contributions have been instrumental in our success. Their funding has enabled us to purchase equipment, build innovative robot designs, and participate in competitions. With their help, we've been able to provide hands-on learning experiences for students, fostering creativity, teamwork, and engineering skills.
We truly value their commitment to inspiring the next generation of technology leaders.
